## How to Subscribe to a Mailing List 

1. If you have never subscribed to a mailing list before, please follow the steps below:
   1. Click on the name of the mailing list you wish to subscribe to.
   2. Your browser will redirect to the subscription page of that mailing list, where instructions on how to subscribe will be provided.
   3. Read the subscription instructions, and you will need to provide an email address that you wish to use for subscribing to the mailing list.
   4. Enter your email address and click subscribe. You will then receive an email requesting you to confirm your subscription.
   5. Reply to the email you received to confirm your subscription.
   6. Finally, you will receive a welcome email from the mailing list.
